# Privacy Policy — Bol (Lyrics for YouTube)

_Last updated: July 5, 2026_

Bol is a Chrome extension that displays synced song lyrics beside YouTube music videos.

## What Bol does with data

- **Song identification.** To find lyrics for the video you are watching, Bol sends the
  video's **title, channel name, and duration** to public lyrics databases
  ([lrclib.net](https://lrclib.net) and music.163.com). This is the only information
  ever transmitted, and it is only sent while you are on a YouTube watch page.
- **Preferences.** Your display settings (panel width, collapsed state, script mode)
  are stored using Chrome's built-in storage sync, tied to your own browser profile.
  They never leave Google's Chrome sync infrastructure.
- **Caching.** Lyrics for a video are cached in Chrome's session storage on your
  device and are cleared when the browser closes.

## What Bol does NOT do

- No personal information is collected, stored, or transmitted.
- No browsing history is read or recorded. Bol only runs on youtube.com watch pages.
- No analytics, tracking pixels, or telemetry of any kind.
- No accounts, no sign-in.
- No data is ever sold or shared with third parties beyond the lyric lookups
  described above.
